Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2025  ·  Sharon's Accounting Service ABN 44 869 154 258

Sharon's Accounting Service is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Who we are

Sharon's Accounting Service (ABN 44 869 154 258) is the operator of the accounting portal at portal.sharonogier.com. We are based in New South Wales, Australia and are subject to the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

Questions about this policy can be directed to: info@sharonogier.com

2. What information we collect

Type of information Examples Why we collect it
Account information Email address, password (encrypted) To create and manage your account
Business profile Business name, ABN, address, phone, logo To populate your invoices and documents
Financial data Invoices, expenses, income sources, GST figures To provide accounting features
Client data Client names, emails, ABNs, addresses To link invoices and quotes to clients
Payment information Billing details (processed by Stripe) To process your subscription payment
Usage data Log data, IP address, browser type To maintain security and improve the service

We do not collect sensitive information such as health records, religious beliefs or biometric data.

3. How we use your information

We use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Providing and improving the portal and its features
  • Processing your subscription payments
  • Sending transactional emails (invoices, password resets, account notices)
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or security threats

We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Who we share your information with

We share your information only with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (data stored in secure cloud infrastructure)
  • Stripe — payment processing (governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Render — cloud hosting and server infrastructure

All third-party providers are required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws. We do not share your data with any other parties unless required by Australian law.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is protected by Row Level Security — meaning only you can access your own records.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access. However, no internet transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your data may be stored on servers located outside of Australia. Where this occurs, we take steps to ensure the data is protected in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

6. Your client data

When you enter personal information about your clients (such as their name, email and ABN) into the portal, you become responsible for ensuring that information is collected and handled lawfully. You should:

  • Have a lawful basis for collecting and storing your clients' personal information
  • Inform your clients how their information is used
  • Comply with your own obligations under the Australian Privacy Act 1988

7. Cookies and tracking

The portal uses essential cookies and browser local storage to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking tools.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the portal.

8. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@sharonogier.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your account, your data will be retained for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted, unless we are required by law to retain it longer.

10. Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by displaying a notice in the portal. The updated policy will take effect 14 days after notification.
